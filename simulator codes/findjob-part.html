    Perhaps you want to work part-time but don’t necessarily want to work a traditional job sitting in an office or standing behind a cash register. If this is the case for you, a part-time job as a tutor might be a good alternative.

    Are you an expert in a particular subject? If so, start advertising your services and looking for opportunities to work with students who are currently struggling. – $33.80 per hour



    Waiter/Waitress

    Waiters and waitresses are essential staff members at restaurants of all kinds. They take orders, deliver food and drinks to people’s tables, and make sure that customers have a good dining experience.

    A job as a waiter or waitress is often quite busy and requires you to be on your feet for hours at a time. However, it’s great for those who prefer a fast-paced environment. – $29.03 per hour

    Cashier

    Cashiers work in restaurants, retail shops, grocery stores, and a variety of other businesses. They ring up people’s purchases, answer questions, and help customers have a good experience.

    A cashier spends a lot of time on their feet, so you need to have enough endurance to be able to handle this. You must have good customer service skills, too. – $24.55 per hour

    Delivery Driver

    If you’re interested in a part-time job that allows you to drive and spend plenty of time alone, another option to consider is a job as a delivery driver.

    Delivery drivers often work for restaurants and are responsible for bringing food orders to customers in a timely manner. Some businesses provide a vehicle for you to use, but others require you to have your own reliable vehicle. – $27.43 per hour

    Retail Sales Assistant

    As a retail sales assistant, you will work in a retail store (such as a clothing store) and help customers find items, make recommendations, and create a pleasant shopping environment. You will likely help with tasks like dressing mannequins or setting up sales displays, too.

    This is a very active job, so it’s great for people who want to stay on their feet. It provides a lot of opportunities for you to work on your customer service skills as well. – $21.13 per hour
