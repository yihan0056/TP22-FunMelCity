Go Back to the airport

A taxi ride to Melbourne from the Airport takes about 30 minutes.It may be the best option to get comfortably to Melbourne’s city center and to your hotel even though one of the most expensive.

Taxi ranks are located across the ground floor of the each terminal.

As in all cases, there’s a special surcharge for trips from the airport (A$4.50). Trip to Melbourne CBD is estimated to cost around A$60.00 - $65.00. It is expected you to pay before arriving at destination so be sure to have your wallet on hand.

Uber, Shebah, Didi and Go Catch are available at Melbourne Airport. In order to ask for a car please download the application to your smartphone.
